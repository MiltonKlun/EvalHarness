# Meridian Robotics — Company Overview

Meridian Robotics is a fictional company invented for this test harness. It was
founded in 2019 in Tromsø, Norway, by Dr. Ada Solvang and Henrik Vale. The company
designs autonomous inspection drones for offshore wind farms.

Meridian's headquarters are in Tromsø, with a second engineering office in Aberdeen,
Scotland, opened in 2022. As of 2024 the company employs 140 people across the two
sites.

The company's mission statement is: "Keep humans off the turbine, keep the turbines
turning." Meridian is privately held and has never disclosed annual revenue publicly.
