# Meridian Robotics — Product Line

Meridian sells two drone models and one software product.

## Kestrel-1
The Kestrel-1 is Meridian's entry-level inspection drone, released in 2020. It has a
flight time of 38 minutes on a single charge and a maximum wind tolerance of 14 metres
per second. The Kestrel-1 carries a 20-megapixel visible-light camera but no thermal
imaging.

## Kestrel-2
The Kestrel-2, released in 2023, is the flagship model. It extends flight time to 55
minutes and raises wind tolerance to 22 metres per second. The Kestrel-2 adds a thermal
imaging camera alongside the visible-light camera, allowing it to detect overheating
components inside turbine nacelles.

## TurbineSight
TurbineSight is Meridian's cloud software for reviewing inspection footage. It runs a
defect-detection model that flags blade cracks, lightning strike damage, and corrosion.
TurbineSight is sold on an annual subscription; pricing is negotiated per customer and
is not published.
